# Madison for HR — Agentic HR for Banks

The people work in your bank, run end to end. Governed agents run the work
between the decisions — candidate intake and scoring, an early screening call,
an employee desk that acts in your HRMS, and exit through offboarding — while
your team keeps every regulated call. Deployed in your perimeter.

## What Madison delivers

- **Cleaner shortlists, faster** — every applicant scored against the role with the reasoning written out; your recruiter advances, always.
- **A help desk that acts** — answers policy from your handbook with the source cited, then completes the task in the HRMS.
- **Exits that close with evidence** — captures why someone left, runs the handover, and proves access was withdrawn.

## Governance

Every regulated decision stays with a person; the agent prepares it and hands it
over with reasoning attached. On-prem or air-gapped; EU AI Act aware;
exportable audit trail.
