# Madison for Commercial Banking

Grow the commercial book without replacing your core. Madison is the agentic AI
layer for commercial banking: more revenue capacity per relationship manager,
faster and more consistent credit decisions, and portfolio risk you can see
early — running on the systems you already run. Live in weeks.

## Workspaces

- **RM Copilot** — decision-ready client briefs, meeting capture, and follow-through.
- **Credit** — gather information, prepare analysis, coordinate decisions with context intact.
- **Client Service** — route requests and coordinate resolution across teams.
- **Portfolio Growth** — surface emerging risk, opportunities, and next-best actions.

## Governance

The banker always decides. Madison prepares inputs; credit, pricing, and
relationship calls stay with your people. Auditable, human-in-the-loop, BYO-LLM,
single-tenant in your region.
