# Madison for Dispute Resolution — Mortgage Servicing

Every servicing dispute answered on time — your team holds the pen. Agents
investigate and draft on your loan data; your team makes the call. The 30-day
RESPA scramble becomes a same-week sign-off. For US banks and credit unions.

## How it stays safe

The model never decides — your rulebook does. Rules score the case, the model
drafts and cites the loan file, the server recomputes the verdict, a person
approves in the Decision Inbox, and everything is logged. RESPA & TRID by
construction; human on every verdict; sovereign / on-prem.

## Two halves of the problem

Prevent (catch the error before the borrower writes in) and resolve (work every
dispute inside the window) — one agentic layer over your data works both ends.
