# Privacy & data handling — Madison by Lyzr

Madison is deployed in your perimeter, trains on nothing you hold, and logs
every action for the exam.

## Where your data lives

Inside your perimeter — on-premises, private cloud (VPC), or fully air-gapped.
Vendor, contract, model, and customer data stays inside your boundary. No
cross-tenant training and no data egress.

## No training on your data

Your data is never used to train a shared model. Personal data is redacted
before any model call, and inference runs against the LLM you bring under your
keys and logging.

## What we record

Every agent action and human decision is written to an exportable,
tamper-evident audit trail — what was read, what was done, and why. You control
retention and export.

## Human accountability

No regulated step is taken autonomously. A person approves every regulated
action.

## Security posture

Aligned to SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001; SSO/SAML and role-based access control;
encrypted in transit and at rest. A full security & compliance pack is available
on request.
