# Madison for Marketing — Compliant Marketing

Madison for Marketing turns a one-line brief into a compliant, launch-ready
campaign, built on your own data, with a human approving every step. Campaigns
that took weeks now take days.

## What it does

One line in; a segmented, multi-channel campaign out — already checked against
your compliance rulebook, with fair-lending guardrails and Reg DD disclosures
inserted before you see the draft. A marketer approves, then a compliance
officer approves, independently. Nothing regulated sends itself.

## Governance

Runs inside your perimeter, aligned to SR 11-7 and GLBA, with an exportable
audit trail of what each agent read, what it did, and why. Value in week one,
no integration to start.
