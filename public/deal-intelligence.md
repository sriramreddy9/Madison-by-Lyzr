# Madison / Spotlight — Deal Intelligence for Banks

Evaluate companies, partners, targets, and pitch opportunities with evidence,
context, and institutional memory. Madison / Spotlight turns fragmented market
data, internal documents, relationship history, and prior evaluations into
decision-ready intelligence for CorpDev, Strategy, Ventures, Innovation,
Partnerships, and Investment Banking.

## How it works

One brief in; a decision-ready output out. Madison builds the company profile,
pulls external market context, maps the opportunity to your strategy, checks
prior evaluations and relationships, surfaces risks, and recommends **partner /
invest / acquire / monitor / pass** — with a memo, evidence pack, or
pitch-style deck, human approval, and permissioned deal memory.

## Governance

Explainable, permissioned, and reviewable — designed for regulated banking
environments (SOC 2, TPRM, model-risk, data residency).
