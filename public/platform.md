# Madison platform — architecture, integration & deployment

Madison runs on a single governed foundation: a shared data layer, agent
orchestration, and deployment inside your perimeter. The same platform powers
every solution.

## Architecture

- **Architect & Studio** — compose agents and workflows for your institution from your rulebook, brand kit, product catalog, approval chains, and history.
- **Sovereign / Optimus** — the runtime, deployed in your perimeter (on-prem, VPC, or air-gapped). Bring your own LLM; inference and data stay in your boundary.
- **Control Plane** — autonomy policy bands (assist, recommend, automate), human-approval gates on every regulated step, and a tamper-evident, exportable audit ledger.

## Integration

Madison reads across your systems of record and files results back into the
tools you already use. Connectors and API access are provisioned during an
engagement — there is no public self-serve API.

- Core banking, servicing platforms, and LOS (read as a layer, never replaced)
- CRM — Salesforce, Dynamics 365, nCino, HubSpot
- Productivity & documents — Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Box, Google Drive
- Data — Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, and modern data lakes
- Send & service — Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Braze, Banno, ServiceNow, Jira
- Identity — SSO / SAML, role-based access control
