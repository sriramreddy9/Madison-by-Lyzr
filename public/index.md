# Madison — The Agentic Banking OS by Lyzr

Madison helps run your bank. It is the Agentic Banking OS by Lyzr: governed AI
agents across every banking function, deployed inside your perimeter with a
human in the loop and a full, exportable audit trail.

Madison is an orchestration layer on top of the core, CRM, and systems you
already run — additive, never a rip-and-replace. Agents assist, recommend, or
automate up to the decision; your people make every regulated decision.

## Solutions by team

- **[Commercial Banking](/commercial-banking-rm)** — relationship management for the commercial book: decision-ready client briefs, credit coordination, and portfolio growth.
- **[Compliant Marketing](/compliant-marketing)** — a one-line brief becomes a compliant, launch-ready campaign on your own data, with a human approving every step.
- **[Deal Intelligence](/deal-intelligence)** — evaluate companies, partners, and targets with evidence, context, and permissioned deal memory.
- **[Dispute Resolution](/dispute-resolution)** — mortgage-servicing disputes investigated and drafted inside the RESPA window; your team signs off.
- **[HR](/hr)** — the employee journey run end to end, with every regulated decision left to your team.
- **[Risk & Compliance](/risk-compliance)** — third-party risk, vendor-AI governance, and tested vendor exits, on-prem and examiner-ready.

Coming soon: Retail Banking, Underwriting, KYC, and Reconciliations.

## Why Madison

- **In your perimeter.** On-premises, private cloud, or air-gapped. Your data never leaves your boundary; no training on your data.
- **Human in the loop.** No autonomous sends or shadow decisions. Every regulated step is gated by a human approval.
- **Examiner-ready.** Every agent action and human decision is cited, timestamped, and exportable.
- **Bring your own LLM.** Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, or private models. Your keys, your logs, your controls.
